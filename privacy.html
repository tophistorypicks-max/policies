Privacy Policy

Top History Picks  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Short version: This Tool does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users or followers. It is a personal automation project with no user-facing login, registration, or data collection of any kind.

1. Who We Are

Top History Picks is a personal, non-commercial automation project that generates and publishes history-themed content to social media platforms. It is not a company, business, or commercial service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal data. This Tool has no website, app, or interface through which users interact directly. There are no sign-up forms, contact forms, comment sections, newsletter subscriptions, or any other mechanism by which personal information is solicited or collected.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any form of user identification or behavioural tracking on this policy page or any page associated with this Tool.

3. How Content Is Published

The Tool operates autonomously using official APIs provided by third-party social media platforms (including Instagram and TikTok). It accesses only the operator's own accounts and credentials. No follower data, profile information, or engagement data from third parties is accessed, stored, or processed beyond what the platforms themselves surface to account owners.

4. Third-Party Platforms

When you interact with content published by this Tool on Instagram, TikTok, or any other platform, your interactions are governed by the privacy policies of those respective platforms. This Tool has no control over, and bears no responsibility for, how those platforms handle your data.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those platforms directly:

· Instagram / Meta: privacycenter.instagram.com/policy
· TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any personal data with third parties because we do not collect any personal data in the first place.

6. Data Retention

No personal data is retained by this Tool. Operational data (such as API tokens and content queues) is stored locally by the operator solely for the purpose of running the automation and is never shared externally.

7. Children's Privacy

This Tool does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). As no data is collected from any users, this applies equally to minors.

8. Your Rights

Because no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, correct, export, or delete. If you have concerns about content published by this Tool, please reach out via the associated social media account.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revisions. Continued interaction with content produced by this Tool constitutes acknowledgement of the current policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please get in touch via the social media accounts associated with Top History Picks.
