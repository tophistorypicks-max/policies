About

Top History Picks is a content management platform designed to help history-focused creators manage the end-to-end workflow of producing and publishing short-form content. From topic research through to scheduling and analytics, the platform streamlines the creation pipeline for TikTok and Instagram.

The platform integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API to enable creators to schedule and publish video content directly to their TikTok accounts, without leaving their workflow.
